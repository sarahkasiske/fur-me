Welcome to Fur Me

Are you interested in owning a dog or learning more about different dog breeds? With Fur Me you can, explore the stipulations of owning a dog, learn about different dog breeds, understand the benefits of adoption or buying from a breeder and prepare to bring a dog home.

Millions of dogs are placed in shelters every year

Of the dogs brought to shelters, the majority are less than a year old, almost half had not been neutered/spayed, a third had never been to the vet and almost none, 96%, had no obedience training. Almost a third of these animals had been given to the owner by a friend but many were from breeders or shelters. A dog can be a wonderful addition to your family but make an educated decision, this should be a life long decision.

Learn How to Ensure Your Home Is a Dogs Forever Home

Before deciding to add a dog to your family do the research, and make a choice that is best for you, your family and the dog you plan to bring home. Add a dog to your family for life, not until they chew up your favorite pair of shoes.

Think about the dog you are choosing to be part of your family. Learning about dog breeds gives you insight into the characteristic of the dogs you are considering, this helps even with a mixed breed dog. Big, small, quiet, energetic, protective and friendly are all characteristics of different breeds. While nature contributes to many characteristics, nurture or how a dog is raised and trained also has influence on the dog your puppy becomes.
