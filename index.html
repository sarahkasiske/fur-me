Welcome to Fur Me

If you are considering adding a dog to your family, Fur Me is a good place start. With Fur Me, you can explore the possibility of owning a dog, learn about different dog breeds, understand the benefits of adoption or buying from a breeder and have a better understanding of the commitment of dog ownership. Fur Me helps you to find a dog that matches the needs of your family and lifestyle and prepare to welcome a new dog into your home.

Millions of Dogs are Placed in Shelters Every Year

Of the dogs brought to shelters, the majority are less than a year old, almost half have not been neutered/spayed, a third have never been to the vet and 96%, had no obedience training. Almost a third of these animals had been given to the owner by a friend but many were from breeders or shelters. A dog can be a wonderful addition to your family but it is important to make an educated decision, choosing to own a dog should be a long-term decision.

Make Sure Your Home Is a Dogs Forever Home

Before deciding to add a dog to your family do the research, and make a choice that is best for you, your family and the dog you plan to bring home. Add a dog to your family for life, not until they chew up your favorite pair of shoes.

Think about the dog you are choosing to be part of your family. Learning about dog breeds gives you insight into the characteristics of the dogs you are considering, this helps even with a mixed breed dog. Big, small, quiet, energetic, protective and friendly are all characteristics of different breeds. While nature contributes to many characteristics, nurture or how a dog is raised and trained also has influence on the dog your puppy becomes.
